Neuroplasticity


1) Discuss how understanding the principles of Neuroplasticity benefits people:

Neuroplasticity is the science, that through the process of repeating a task or set of tasks we are able to commit it to memory. the reason this is an important benfit to us as individuals is that it allows us to keep learning and growing our understanding no matter how old we get, it just might take a little more time with age.

Growth Mindset


1) Discuss what it is and why it is relevant:

Growth mindset is a theory centred around the belief that intelligence and learning can be developed and improved. If someone has a growth mindset, they have a positive attitude towards learning and their ability to progress and achieve or in other terms they see challenges as a way to gorw ande become better instead of as a road block.
